Email Blaster Workflow

The Email Blaster works in a three-step process:

  • 1. Create your new email blast. Click the "Create New Email" button and write your new email blast. If you wish to continue creating the email blast, you may save it using the "Save" button and continue at a later time. Once done, click the "Confirm" button to send the email blast request to all administrators.
  • 2. Confirm the email blast details with two administrators. Wait for two administrators to confirm the contents of the email blast. Once the confirmations are received, their names will appear as confirmers and the "Submit" button will be enabled. However, if any one administrator denies the email blast, the email blast will be transferred over to the "Denied Email Blasts" section where you'll have to edit the email and wait for confirmation from administrators once again.
  • 3. Submit the email to the specified clients. In the submit page you're able to input the csv file of client emails receiving the email blast. Make sure the csv file follows the correct format as shown in the page. Once the csv file is added, click "submit" to finally send out the email blast.
